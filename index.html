ABOUT

Studio Tenwu is reimagining K-12 EdTech using agentic AI. We're building an AI Engineering environment for children, integrated into games they already play like Minecraft and Roblox. Kids build their own AI agents, use them in-game, and eventually export them to interact with the real world. We're making AI Engineering as fundamental to education as Math, Science, or English.
